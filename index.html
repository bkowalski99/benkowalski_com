Who Am I?

I grew up in Cincinnati, Ohio and went to THE Ohio State University for College. In college I studied Computer Engineering with a focus in Computer Architecture.

During this time I was lucky enough to act as a recruitment lead for OHI/O, our college's hackathon program, as well as work as a Machine Learning researcher with the Computational Combustion and Energy Group here at Ohio State.

What Do I Do?
